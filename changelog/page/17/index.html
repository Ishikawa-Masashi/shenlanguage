    We now dynamically attach the data-theme attribute to chakra elements when in forced color mode.

    <DarkMode>
      <chakra.div bg='gray.800' padding='40px'>
        {/* Forced: Badge will now have `data-theme='dark' attached` */}
        <Badge>Total</Badge>
      </chakra.div>
    </DarkMode>
